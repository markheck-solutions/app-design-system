# Decision Records

Use `templates/DESIGN_DECISION_RECORD.md` when a visual, token, workflow, Figma, or rollout decision should survive across apps.

Decision records should be short and concrete:

- What changed.
- Why it changed.
- Alternatives considered.
- Consequences.
- Affected tokens, patterns, or apps.

Store accepted decision records in a future `docs/decisions/` folder when needed.
